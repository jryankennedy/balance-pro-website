Introducing Balance, iOS App

The simple way to track your banking transactions.

April 18, 2014 by Jordan Kennedy


Today, I’m introducing Balance, a new app that allows you to quickly add transactions to your bank accounts and keeps your checkbook balanced.

Balance features multiple bank accounts, multiple currencies, the ability to clear transactions, export to a spreadsheet and add a passcode to protect your information. You may use Balance in portrait or landscape, whatever’s most comfortable for you.

Balance is iAd supported, but there is an in-app purchase for $1.99 to remove the advertisements in the app's settings.

This app is available for free in the app store on iOS 7 or later starting April 18, 2014. Directed and designed by Jordan Kennedy. Developed by Alex Butenko.

Why should I use Balance?

Many purchases don’t immediately post to your bank account which result in pending transactions. Those pending transaction amounts can change when it posts to, or clears, your account.

For example, let’s say you swipe your credit card at a gas station and fill your vehicle’s tank. The gas station initially charges you $1 to check if your account is in good standing. Later, the amount clears for $20. During this pending time, your balance is off by $19. A few transactions like these can add up quickly leaving you confused about your true account balance.

With balance, simply enter your purchase and the app will immediately subtract the full amount so you know exactly how much money you have left to spend.
